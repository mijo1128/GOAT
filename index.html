Max Holloway

The Greatest of All Time

Rallying after a bitter loss

Holloway's professional career had an inconsistent start in the UFC with loses to Dustin Poirier and a young Conor McGregor.

After losing to McGregor, Holloway would go on to have a record setting 13-fight winning streak.

In this historic run, Holloway defeated a several elite fighters, such as Anthony Pettis, Brian Ortega, Cub Swanson, future lightweight champion Charles Oliveira, and one of the most feared fighters in UFC History, Jose Aldo.

Defeating a legend

After a historic winning winning streak, Max Holloway was granted a title shot where he would fight Jose Aldo. After some adversity, Holloway landed a clean hit from which Jose Aldo would not recover leading him to capture the UFC Featherweight belt.

On February of 2017, Max fought Jose Also for the second time on short notice. Upon hearing the news, Holloway reacted with a simple "it is what it is" which he adopted as his saying during his future victories.

Best boxer in the UFC

The UFC returned to live TV on January 16, 2021 for the first time since December, 2018.

This was an event of the upmost importance since the UFC was its debut on ABC and it was the first time in almost a year where audiences were allowed to attend.

In the final round of the fight Max looked way from his opponent and began yelling towards the the commentary table "I'm the best boxer in the UFC!" all while dodging multiple strikes from Calvin Kattar who is one of the most accurate fighters in the division.

Holloway and Kattar delivered a main event where several records where shattered.

"Daddest man on the planet"

Max Holloway's fans have given him the title of "Daddest man on the planet". This is a play on "The baddest man on the planet" which is an unofficial title given to the UFC Heavyweight champion.

Max Holloway has brought his son, Rush, to all his UFC fights and will get chocolate drunk after every one of his fights win or lose.

He is also seen constantly at his son's event supporting and cheering him on and are rarely far from each other.
